NaturalMan

The mission of this superhero is to save the environment and punish the people who pollute the environment. The powers of this NaturalMan increases whenever he do truthful thing he is normal like any other people but whenever he wants he can shift to superhero face by saying a poem in mind.

He can travel from one place to other place in few seconds with help of environment like water,air,sand,trees.
